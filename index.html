Frequently Asked Questions

Show FAQs
What if the clips tank?
You pay nothing. Escrow auto-refunds.
What if you miss the 72-hour window?
We produce two additional clips free—documented in the contract.
What does “risk-free pilot” actually mean?
We edit three Shorts from your footage. If any clip fails to beat your 30-day average by ≥40%, escrowed funds auto-refund. If we miss our 72-hour delivery window, your next two clips are free—written in the contract.
Why doesn’t my long-form content perform as well anymore?
Algorithms now surface 15–60 s clips first. Viewers decide in 3 seconds whether to stick around. We isolate those “hook moments” inside your existing videos and push them where the algorithm wants—so your long-form gets rediscovered instead of ignored.
I already have an editor—why bring in your team?
Solo editors cut clips; we run a three-layer system: hook analysis, format templates, and A/B metadata testing. You keep your editor for long-form polish while we drive Shorts performance.
Will this add work to my plate?
No logins, no manual exports. You drop raw footage in Drive; within 72 h you get finished clips + metadata. Post them or let us post— your workflow shrinks, not expands.
